About us

At Glamour Nail Lounge, we understand that everyone has different needs, therefore we offer a unique one to one service for our clients to ensure they receive individual advice and treatments best suited to them.

We pride ourselves on providing our customers with fabulously indulgent nail care while maintaining the highest level of cleanliness and sterilization. To ensure you are protected, all of our equipment is UV sterilized and all small tools are one-time use only.

Our team is constantly seeking out new brands and services we believe our clients will enjoy.

We are also the perfect party venue for birthday, bachelorette and holiday celebrations.

STORE POLICY


Guarantee:

We offer a 2 days guarantee on our regular manicures and a 7 days guarantee on our Shellac Manicures. Should your manicure chip within this time frame, we will fix them free of charge.


Appointments/ Walk-in

Although we highly recommend appointments for your convenience but walk-ins are welcome depending upon our availability.

Please note we allow a 10 min grace period for all appointments. If you are running late, please contact our front desk to reschedule, otherwise your appointment will be cancelled.


Gift cards

Gift cards are available for purchase in store. The perfect gift for that special someone!
